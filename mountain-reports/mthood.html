Surface Conditions

Groom is combination of hard pack and soft pack. Off piste is a dusting of fresh over skier-packed powder / hard pack. Be aware of hazards and unmarked obstacles, and tree litter. No uphill traffic in the resort. Please use the hiking routes outside the boundaries.

General Comments

A dusting of snow overnight added a touch of freshness to the trails, and there is sunshine in the forecast to add a touch of softness later. Freezing precipitation overnight helped some areas get a little "extra" firm, but after a little while in the sun today those solid spots will start to get more forgiving, making for a day of spring-like conditions on the slopes. There is a touch of ice on surfaces as well this morning, so watch your step in the parking lot and please be patient as our crews work to de-ice equipment. As we kick off the final Wednesday of Meadows After Dark for the season, we know we are going to miss these late nights under the lights. Join us and make the most of your Wednesday! Meadows runs a guest shuttle from Hood River every day for FREE. For details visit Skihood.com/shuttle. We'll see you soon!
