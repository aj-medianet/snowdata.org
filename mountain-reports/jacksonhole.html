9:43 Sublette temporarily closed to the public due to high winds.

8:56 The Mountain is now open. All lifts are open.

The mountains will see breezy conditions today as moderate winds will consistently trend out of the West at 25-35 MPH with gusts in the high elevations reaching into the 50 MPH range. We will see a warming trend over the next couple of days with today's temperatures reaching the mid-20's on the Upper Mountain while the base area will see temps creep into the low 40's. There is a slight chance for snow showers this morning with mostly cloudy skies throughout the day. Ski conditions will be fast in the morning and will start to soften as the day progresses. Note on Mountain Weather

This snow and weather report is for Teton Village, Wyoming and Jackson Hole Mountain Resort. Snowfall information is obtained from directly from the snow study plots maintained by the Bridger-Teton National Forest Avalanche Forecast Center. Weather information is obtained from NOAA and MountainWeather.com, but weather patterns in Jackson Hole are exceedingly difficult to predict - when venturing into the mountains, be prepared for rapid, dramatic weather changes.
